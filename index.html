WHO ARE WE

Jessin Farms is a Zambian company established in the year 2012 and registered in 2015 with PACRA. The aim of the company is to provide quality and internationally acceptable products to the consumers. In addition, the company aims at working hand in hand with the stake holders in order for the business to grow and execute all of the company’s programs and plans.

×

WHERE ARE WE

Jessin Farms is a small company that is currently involved in the production and supply of organic foods to the general public. The company is currently embarking on projects of expanding its businesses by building poultry’s and clearing more land for its Horticulture projects.
